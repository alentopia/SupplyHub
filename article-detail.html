2026 Trend: Big Restaurants Are Bypassing Middlemen & Going Direct to Farmers!

Bima Adhitya Agri-Business Analyst

Middlemen are slowly fading out. Huge F&B corps and supermarkets now prefer direct contracts with local farmers. Thicker margins, safer supply.

This shift isn't just about cutting costs; it's a fundamental restructuring of the food supply chain. By leveraging modern digital platforms like TaniDirect, regional restaurant chains can now forecast their produce needs months in advance and secure reliable harvests directly from the source.

Farmers benefit equally, enjoying guaranteed offtake agreements and faster payment cycles. The traditional wholesale markets, while still present, are seeing a decline in premium, high-volume transactions as transparency and traceability become paramount for end consumers.
